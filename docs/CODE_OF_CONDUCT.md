## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct which could be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.
